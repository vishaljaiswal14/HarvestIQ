Optimal wheat nutrition requires nitrogen between 200-400 kg/ha soil available N depending on soil test, phosphorus 12-25 kg/ha, and potassium 150-300 kg/ha for Indian alluvial soils.

Soil pH should remain between 6.0 and 7.5 for maximum nutrient uptake. Organic carbon below 0.40 percent indicates need for organic matter supplementation through FYM or green manure.

Split nitrogen application: one-third at sowing, one-third at crown root initiation, and remainder before flowering per ICAR package of practices.
