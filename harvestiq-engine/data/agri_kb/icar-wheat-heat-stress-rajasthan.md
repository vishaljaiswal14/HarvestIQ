Wheat crops in Rajasthan face significant heat stress during late Rabi season when temperatures exceed 35°C during grain filling.

Farmers should schedule light irrigation during peak afternoon heat to reduce canopy temperature. ICAR recommends avoiding nitrogen top-dressing when forecast maximum temperature exceeds 38°C for three consecutive days.

KVK Bharatpur and nearby districts advise mulching in irrigated wheat to conserve soil moisture. Monitor crop stage using GDD accumulation to time protective irrigation before flowering.

State agriculture department bulletins recommend early-morning field scouting when thermal stress indices are elevated.
