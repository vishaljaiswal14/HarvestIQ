Rice blast causes diamond-shaped lesions with gray centers on leaves. High humidity and moderate temperatures favor blast spread during Kharif season.

State agriculture department recommends balanced nitrogen application because excess nitrogen increases blast susceptibility. Maintain proper plant spacing for air circulation.

KVK field schools demonstrate seed treatment and timely drainage to reduce blast incidence in lowland rice.
