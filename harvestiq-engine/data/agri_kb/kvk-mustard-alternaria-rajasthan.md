Alternaria blight on mustard shows dark concentric spots on leaves and pods. The disease is common in Rajasthan during humid Rabi periods after light rainfall.

Farmers should use certified disease-free seed and practice field sanitation. State advisories recommend fungicide application at first symptom appearance on lower leaves.

Crop rotation with cereals reduces inoculum carryover for subsequent mustard seasons.
