Wheat rust appears as orange-brown pustules on leaves and stems. Early detection during tillering and flowering stages is critical for Rajasthan and Indo-Gangetic plains.

Government advisories recommend removing heavily infected leaves and avoiding overhead irrigation that spreads spores. Apply recommended fungicide only when rust severity exceeds economic threshold and weather is not windy.

Integrated pest management guidelines from ICAR stress crop rotation and resistant varieties for long-term rust control.
